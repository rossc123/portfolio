I am currently studying 3D Design for Virtual Environments at Glasgow Caledonian University’s School of Computing, Engineering and Built Environment. I study 3D modeling, animation and texturing as well as video and motion graphics.

On the Master’s programme there is a wide range of students from different backgrounds and disciplines, working effectively together. We are currently working in teams to develop a 3D visualisation project.

This semester I am personally focusing on two areas. The first is to create a fully developed character beginning with the design and research stage and concluding with a fully modelled and textured character ready to be rigged for animation.
My second task is to create game level in the Unity engine using only assets I have created. Through this I will further my knowledge of the professional workflow used in the games industry and learn how to deliver projects for multiple platforms.

I am currently in the proposal stage of my Master’s project which is heavily influenced by video game designer Fumito Ueda and his philosophy of design by subtraction. I intend to apply aspects of this philosophy to virtual reality to increase player immersion.
